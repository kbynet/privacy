# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: February 20, 2026*

## Overview

This document describes how information is collected, used, and shared when you use this project.

## Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

- **Usage Data**: Information about how you interact with this project, such as log data and diagnostic information.
- **Account Information**: If you contribute or sign in, we may collect your username and email address.

## How We Use Information

Collected information is used to:

- Operate and improve the project.
- Respond to issues, pull requests, and support requests.
- Understand how the project is being used.

## Information Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except:

- When required by law.
- To protect the rights and safety of users and contributors.

## Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy.

## Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

- Access the personal data we hold about you.
- Request correction or deletion of your data.
- Object to or restrict our processing of your data.

To exercise any of these rights, please open an issue in this repository.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be noted by updating the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, by opening a pull request for community review.

## Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue in this repository.
